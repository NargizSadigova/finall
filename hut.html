Løvtag’s three exclusive and comfortable cabins, Et, Ro and Ly, are designed by architect Sigurd Larsen. They all have an open space with a double bed, a double sofa bed, kitchen, separate toilet and an outdoor shower. On the roof, surrounded by treetops, there is a terrace, which is about nine meters above ground. The cottages are built around tall, old trees that go through the entire cottage from floor to ceiling.

The fully equipped kitchen allows you to prepare delicious meals with ease. You'll find everything you need, including a refrigerator, stovetop, and a selection of utensils and cookware. The interiors reflect a minimalist yet cozy design, ensuring that the natural light and the views outside are the main attraction. The warmth of the wood and the clean lines of the furniture create a harmonious and welcoming space.

The bathroom is designed to offer a spa-like experience. It includes a spacious shower area and eco-friendly toiletries, adding a touch of sustainability to your stay. Large windows invite natural light, while maintaining your privacy with frosted glass panels. This thoughtful design ensures that your connection to nature is never compromised, even while indoors.
